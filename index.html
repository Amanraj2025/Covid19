Child Vaccination

  • Covid vaccine is now available for Children of the age group 12+ yrs in Vaccination Centre. The time span between first and second dose of Covovax is 21 days. Children can be administered with the second dose of Covovax within a month.
  • Children of the age group 12-14 yrs are now eligible for the Covid vaccine in Vaccination Centre. The period between a first and second dose of Corbevax is 28 days.
  • Covid Vaccine is available for Children of the age group of 15+ yrs in both Government and Private Vaccination Centre. Children must be completed 4 to 6 weeks after administration of the first dose of Covid to take the second dose of Covaxin. Both online and walk-in are available.
